Our Story

“If you never did, you should. These things are fun and fun is good”

Dr Seuss

Content discovery made for kids

Starting as a personal project by a father who wanted to create a way for his daughter to safely play games and watch videos online, KIDOZ has become a global network helping millions of kids find content they love and enabling hundreds of brands and developers to monetize and distribute content to kids.

Everyone involved in the kids’ mobile space is facing new challenges these days:

  • Kids are always looking for new games and videos but often end up with irrelevant and inappropriate content.
  • Brands are looking for kids’ engagement, and need to reach an audience once reached on a few TV channels who are now scattered on thousands of apps.
  • App developers who create amazing apps for kids are looking for a way to get compensated for their work.

This trifecta of issues was desperately waiting for a smart & safe solution.

The KIDOZ Network unites all players in one safe platform.

We help app developers and device makers generate revenue from their work in a COPPA compliant way that respects their values, using sponsored content recommendations.

The network we built allows brands to reach millions of engaged kids around the world through one focal point,and just as importantly, helps children discover mobile content and games in a way that is safer and friendlier than ever.
